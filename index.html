The Problem

Retail Traders Are Structurally Unprotected

In volatile digital asset markets, retail participants face sophisticated risks they cannot observe in real time. Institutional tools exist. Retail tools don't. CPX Protocol exists to close this gap — permanently.

Invisible Capital Erosion

Drawdown compounds silently. Most retail traders cannot observe risk accumulation until capital is already lost.

No Behavioral Risk Layer

Existing tools focus on price. None model behavioral patterns, position sizing risk, or cascade scenarios in real time.

Information Asymmetry

Institutional participants access risk intelligence that retail traders simply cannot afford or access.
