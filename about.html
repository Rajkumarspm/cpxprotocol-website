B.Sc Psychology, 2018. No engineering degree. No institutional backing. No co-founder. Three daughters. Night shifts. SHIELD-X.

CPX Protocol began as a personal research question: why do retail traders consistently fail in ways that were, in principle, observable in advance? The answer — lack of real-time behavioral risk intelligence — became the product.

Every module of SHIELD-X was built through AI-assisted development, rigorous self-education, and a commitment to never shipping something that deceives the user. Non-advisory by design. Non-custodial by architecture. Free by choice.

The Psychology background is not incidental. Understanding how traders behave under stress, how loss aversion creates compounding errors, and how information asymmetry distorts decision-making — this is the theoretical foundation of SHIELD-X's behavioral layer.

Vision

CPX Protocol exists to be the economic security layer for digital asset markets. Not a trading tool. Not an exchange. An intelligence layer that sits beneath every retail trader's decision and gives them the risk visibility that institutions have always had.

The long-term architecture envisions SHIELD-X as the client-facing interface of a broader risk intelligence infrastructure: API-licensed to brokers, integrated by fintech platforms, and eventually underpinned by the CPX token as a utility and governance layer — after full regulatory compliance.

The mission is not quick. Compliance-first development is slower than move-fast-and-break-things. But it is the only honest path.

CPX Token — Role in the Ecosystem

CPX is the utility token of CPX Protocol. Currently frozen. Not publicly available. Its role is architectural — not speculative.

Supply

Separation from SHIELD-X

SHIELD-X operates completely independently of CPX. The tool is free and functional with zero token dependency.
